Optio User Agreement

  1. I declare that I am of sound mind and body.
  2. I understand that Optio is not therapy or a substitute for counseling. It is for individuals who are committed to impacting the quality of their own lives and the lives of those around them.
  3. I promise to respect the confidentiality of all members. Specifically, I promise to not gossip or receive gossip about anyone participating in Optio.
  4. I agree to be open to being guided and agree to guiding others.
  5. I agree to be held accountable and hold my partner accountable.
  6. I promise to be on time for accountability calls, affirmation sessions, and transition sessions.
  7. I agree to preserve Optio’s integrity by keeping materials I receive in support of guided accountability within the Optio community.
